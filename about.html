About Us

London Railway Group are industry-leading Rail, Civil Engineering & Construction recruitment agency, providing career opportunities in seven different sectors on both a freelance and permanent basis. We have worked with market-leading contractors as well as regional, national, and multi-national blue-chip clients. This means we can use our well-established presence and reputation to attract and secure clients nationwide with experienced, top-quality candidates. We collaborate with clients to tackle the challenges that we all collectively face; diversification, skill shortage, innovation, sustainability, and corporate social responsibility targets.
