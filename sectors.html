Our Sectors

Civil Engineering

London Railway Group has built a reputation for working in close partnership with its clients to deliver high quality, value led solutions for civil engineering projects. Our experience on sites is managed by a team of dedicated specialists benefiting from a wealth of experience and work.
It goes without saying that compliance, safety and certification are at the forefront of our business. We have a dedicated HSQE Manager to conduct site inspection visits and random substance abuse testing and robust checks to ensure that all operatives have CSCS/ CPCS qualifications.

Construction

London Railway Group has developed close working relationships with its clients to deliver high quality projects safely whilst focusing on value for money. Through our close working relationships with clients we understand how financially focused projects are and how budgets and timings are key drivers.
Working through our network of regional office, the strength of our building team lies in its ability to combine local presence and knowledge with an absolute focus on our clients' requirements. With this standard of service we repeatedly deliver quality construction projects in a safe, professional and timely manner.

Rail

London Railway Group has a specialist rail division that delivers a wide range of solutions specifically to the rail industry.
