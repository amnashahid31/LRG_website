Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how London Railway Group collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information obtained from individuals, including customers, employees, contractors, and website visitors.

Infromation We Collect:

• Name of the applicant.
• Email of the applicant.
• Phone and Postal Code of the applicant.
• Password of the applicant.
• CV/Resume of the applicant.

How We Use Your Information:

We process personal data for the following purposes:
• To fulfill the recruitment process conveniently.
• To comply with legal obligations.
• For general communication and direct marketing.
• For improvement and analysis of our Products through reviews.
• For legitimate interests pursued by London Railway Group or a third party with consent where required.

Till when is your Information Retained:

Your personal information will be removed once it is no longer necessary for the intended purposes, and there are no legal obligations or other valid reasons to retain it.

The policy is reviewed and updated as needed and it is in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation Standards.
